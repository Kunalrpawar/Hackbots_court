About Us

Our Mission

The Commercial Court is dedicated to providing efficient, fair, and expert resolution of complex business disputes. We strive to maintain the highest standards of justice while fostering a business-friendly legal environment.

Our History

Established in 1995, the Commercial Court has been at the forefront of business law for over two decades. Our experienced judges and staff have handled thousands of cases, contributing to the stability and growth of our business community.

Our Expertise

Our court specializes in a wide range of commercial disputes, including contract disagreements, intellectual property rights, corporate governance issues, and international trade conflicts. We continuously update our knowledge and procedures to stay current with evolving business practices and laws.
